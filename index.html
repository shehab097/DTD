DIG THE DATA 3.0

"Dig The Data" Season 2, hosted by the Niter Computer Club, captivated NITER’s junior batch with an exciting journey into data exploration and problem-solving! Participants showcased remarkable creativity and determination, tackling challenges, uncovering patterns, and solving problems like true detectives. The event wasn’t just about winning—it celebrated learning, collaboration, and growth. Teams pushed their limits, forging friendships and gaining invaluable skills. Congratulations to all participants for making this season a massive success! To the champions, well done! Here's to many more seasons of digging deeper and celebrating the power of data!

Roadmap for 3 Rounds

Round 1: Data Collection

Participants will gather and preprocess data from various sources. This round focuses on understanding the data and preparing it for analysis.

Round 2: Data Analysis

Teams will analyze the collected data to uncover patterns, trends, and insights. This round emphasizes analytical skills and the ability to interpret data.

Round 3: Data Presentation

Participants will present their findings in a clear and compelling manner. This round highlights the importance of communication and visualization skills.
